Johns lake is based in near sandford Berkshire England.It was first dug in the 1980s and has been stocked with a number of different species such as tench pike perch roach and of course carp. The lake is very well know for its carp fishing and in recent years has got a lot of attention with its rather large inhabitants. The current lake record is 50 pound common and mirror with an estimated 400 fish in 90 acres 10 of theses are over forty pound and 100 are over the 30 pound barrier which is an impressive stock of carp. The lake is a park lake and has a lot of dog walker etc as you could image the lake its self is tree lined and has matured into a gorgeous lake for all to enjoy the lake is very weedy and has a depth of around 8 feet deep on average As you can imagine the lake does have a rather large waiting list.
